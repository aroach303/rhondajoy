Her Story

Design Has Always
Been the Language

Rhonda Joy has always seen the world in terms of how spaces make people feel. Long before it was her profession, she was rearranging rooms, curating color palettes, and imagining how environments could better serve the lives lived inside them.

With a background that spans creative industries — including animation and visual storytelling — Rhonda brings a rare, cinematic eye to interior design. She understands composition, light, and the emotional weight of color in ways most designers simply don't.

Today, her work centers on one mission: helping real people — especially those caring for loved ones and building something meaningful — create spaces that reflect who they truly are.

"Your home should be your favorite place in the world. That's not a luxury — it's something everyone deserves."

Listen First
