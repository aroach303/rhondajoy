The Designer Behind the Vision

Meet Rhonda Joy

A creative spirit with a lifelong passion for beauty, color, and the way a space can change how you feel when you walk through the door.

My Story

Design Is How I Say I Care

Rhonda Joy brings a uniquely creative perspective to interior design — one shaped by years working across animation, government projects, and a deep appreciation for visual storytelling. That background taught her something powerful: how a space is designed shapes how people feel inside of it.

Now she channels that same creative energy into homes and spaces for real people — helping clients in Nashville, Tennessee and across the country (virtually) create environments that feel warm, intentional, and genuinely theirs.

Her approach is never about trends. It is about you — your lifestyle, your personality, your comfort — expressed through color, texture, and thoughtful arrangement.
