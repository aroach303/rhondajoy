Click any photo slot below to upload a new image, or drag and drop a photo directly onto a slot. Changes preview instantly right here — your website gallery will reflect the new photos once you upload them to GitHub.

How to Update a Photo

  • 1 Click the Upload Photo button on any slot below, or drag and drop a photo directly onto it.
  • 2 The preview updates instantly so you can see exactly how it will look.
  • 3 Save the photo to your computer using the exact filename shown in the gray box on each slot.
  • 4 Send the renamed photo to your website manager (Austin) and he will upload it to your images/gallery/ folder — your gallery updates automatically.
