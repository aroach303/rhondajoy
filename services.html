Common Questions

What to Expect

How does virtual consulting work?

We connect via video call. You show Rhonda your space in real time, share photos beforehand, and walk away with a personalized design plan — no travel required.

What if I have a tight budget?

Rhonda works with all budgets. Her goal is to maximize what you have and guide you toward impactful changes that don't require spending a fortune.

Do you work with renters?

Absolutely. Rhonda specializes in creating beautiful, personalized spaces within the constraints of a rental — no permanent changes required.

How long does a project take?

A virtual consult can happen within days. Larger projects vary by scope. Rhonda always sets clear timelines upfront so you know what to expect.
